Discord Bots

During high school, there was a 10 player game released call Among Us. My friends and I became extremely interested in the game but sometimes, there would be too many people that wanted to play. So, I took it into my own hands to create a bot using Discord's API that had a queue.

The bot had a few simple commands for joining and leaving that allowed us to keep track of who was in the game.
